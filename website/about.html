About Jeff Burgers

At Jeff Burgers, we believe in crafting the best burgers made with fresh ingredients, passion, and love. Since our founding, our goal has been to provide our customers with an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you're in the mood for a classic burger or something new and exciting, Jeff Burgers has something for everyone!

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Jeff Burgers started with a simple mission: to make burgers better. Our founder, Jeff, always dreamed of creating the perfect burger and decided to turn that dream into a reality. After perfecting the recipes, Jeff Burgers was born. Today, we're proud to serve a community of burger lovers with our unique and delicious menu.

Visit Us at Pandora & Quadra

Our restaurant is conveniently located at the cross-section of Pandora and Quadra Streets in Victoria, BC. Come visit us for a delicious burger and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of our neighborhood.
